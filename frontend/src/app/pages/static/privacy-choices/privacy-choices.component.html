PFTV Privacy Choices

PFTV takes your privacy seriously, so we provide you with a variety of choices regarding how we use the information we collect about you, and how you can manage your online privacy when you use the PFTV Services. Here’s how you can exercise those choices.

If you want more information or have questions, contact us at privacy@projectfitnesstv.com.

To update your user profile information preferences?

  • Log into your account and got to your settings page

To disable or deactivate your PFTV user account

  • Log into your PFTV account and go to your settings page

To close your account and delete your profile information (Note: If you close your account, PFTV may retain other information about you for the activities described in our Privacy Policy, or as permitted or required by applicable law.

  • Email us at privacy@projectfitnesstv.com

To Block users and strangers

  • Log into your PFTV account and go to settings page.

For other request, please email us at privacy@projectfitnesstv.com

Note that we are not legally required to accommodate such requests under all circumstances, which may include if we need to keep processing your information for our legitimate interests or to comply with a legal obligation. We may also request that you provide us with additional information necessary to verify your identity (such as your IP address, date of birth, transaction IDs, and email address) before responding to your request.

PFTV aims to provide the same privacy options regardless of location; however, certain options may differ in accordance with local regulations and requirements. For instance, Residents of the European Economic Area (“EEA”) and California can exercise certain additional rights available to them under applicable data protection laws.
