PFTV Donate

Any funds received by a fitness or nutrition professional on the PFTV website through the DONATE button are not/ and should not be used as payment for goods and services, but are monetary tips to help fitness and nutrition professionals stream, and upload high quality videos. PFTV is thankful for your supporting the fitness and nutrition professionals on our website through viewership. While it is appreciated if you do decide to tip, it is not required. Before tipping, please consider that your tips are non-refundable.

PFTV pays creators 90% of tips after deducting transactions costs. The transaction costs vary based upon price; the calculator uses average transaction per price point.
